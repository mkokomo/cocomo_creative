THE WOMAN

Hi! My name is Mary. I like reading design blogs and the news, listening to TED talks and podcasts, and [most of all] making things. Whether it's designing a logo, coding a website, or building a piece of furniture, creating something with my hands is my favorite thing to do. I have high confidence in my abilities and so do the people that know me. If you have an idea, chances are I can make it happen. So let's chat!
